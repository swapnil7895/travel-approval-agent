# Travel Reimbursement Policy

## 1. Meal Allowance
- Breakfast: ₹200 per day
- Lunch: ₹300 per day
- Dinner: ₹400 per day
- **Daily total meal limit: ₹500 per day** (combined cap, not sum of above)
- Receipt mandatory for claims above ₹200

## 2. Hotel Accommodation
- Metro cities (Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai, Pune): ₹5000 per night
- Non-metro cities: ₹3000 per night
- Receipt mandatory for all hotel claims
- Manager approval required if claim exceeds limit by more than 20%

## 3. Local Transport
- Taxi/Cab: ₹1000 per day
- Auto/Public transport: ₹500 per day
- Receipt required for claims above ₹500

## 4. Air Travel
- Economy class only
- Business class not permitted without VP approval
- Booking must be made minimum 7 days in advance where possible

## 5. Miscellaneous / Other Categories
- Categories not explicitly listed (e.g. team offsites, client entertainment)
  require Manual Review regardless of amount
- No standard limit applies to miscellaneous categories

## 6. General Rules
- Original receipt mandatory for all claims above ₹200
- Claims must be submitted within 30 days of expense date
- Duplicate claims (same employee, date, and amount) will be rejected
- Any claim exceeding 2x the standard limit requires Manual Review
